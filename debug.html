After graduating in 2019 from the Liceo Marconi Delpino in Chiavari, I initially undertook studies as a Laboratory Technician at the University of Genoa.
However, I soon realised that path did not suit me, leading me to reflect on what I really wanted to do in life.
The answer became clear: create video games.

In 2022, I therefore enrolled in the Game Design and Development course at the Vigamus Academy in Rome, moving to the capital to follow my passion. During the course, I was able to delve into different aspects of game design and work on numerous projects.
After obtaining my certification, I started collaborating with former classmates and other professionals to develop Mind Scraper, a graphic adventure in Unity that allows players to discover worlds generated by people's minds.

The project's demo was awarded second place at the third edition of Zagarolo Gamehouse, a competition promoted by the Lazio Region, which allowed us to meet with experts in the field, including professionals from One-O-One Games.
Subsequently, an improved version of the demo was presented at First Playable 2024 in Florence, receiving positive feedback from the public and experts.

In 2025, I also undertook a course to obtain the qualification as Full-Stack Programmer, in order to expand my programming skills and further enhance my professionalism in the field.
